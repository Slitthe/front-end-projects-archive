Hit the Targets

Play Instructions

  • Hit as many targets as possible while avoiding hitting any ducks.
  • Choose the difficulty.
  • After the game starts, normal and duck targets will appear randomly on the screen.
  • Level passing percentages is 75% (non-ducks targets).
  • Hitting a duck has a penality of 1 life. When there are no more lives left, the game is over (lives will reset each level).
  • The targets will appear at an increasingly faster pace as as the levels increase.
