Score keeper

    Usage instructions
  • Enter the score to play until in the input box below.
  • Each time the "Player One" or "Player Two" button is clicked, their score is increased by 1.
  • When either player reaches that score, an alert message will be displayed.
  • To reset the round, press the "reset" button.
